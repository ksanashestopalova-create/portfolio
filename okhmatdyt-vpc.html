The Fit — Value Proposition Statement
For working parents who choose where their child is treated, Okhmatdyt offers the clinical depth of a specialist hospital with the predictability and service standards of a private clinic — at a transparent, fair price.
Unlike private clinics, Okhmatdyt provides access to rare specialists, complex diagnostics, and niche services (neurodevelopmental screening, sports rehabilitation) that cannot be found elsewhere in the region — now delivered through a trustworthy, organised service experience.
